Built for Scale

Sunrise Canada is a premier indoor cannabis flower producer headquartered in Ottawa. We combine meticulous cultivation practices with scalable production infrastructure, delivering consistently exceptional flower to licensed partners and regulated markets around the globe. Every harvest reflects our commitment to quality, compliance, and the highest standards of craft cannabis production.
